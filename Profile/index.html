Ryan Bellmoor

Hi there! I'm Ryan, a dedicated VFX artist with a boundless passion for bringing dreams to life through the magic of visual effects. With a strong foundation in both traditional and digital art, I specialize in creating stunning, cinematic visuals that transport audiences to otherworldly realms. My goal is to continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of VFX and storytelling.


Over 8 years of experience in the entertainment industry, I've had the privilege of contributing my VFX expertise to a range of projects, from feature films and television series to commercials and video games. My work has graced screens both big and small, and I've been fortunate to collaborate with talented teams to bring their creative visions to life.


Proficient in a wide array of VFX tools and software, I excel in areas such as:


  • Particle Effects
  • 3D Animation
  • Matte Painting
  • Compositing
  • Green Screen
  • Simulation
  • Character Animation
  • CGI Integration
